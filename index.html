Introduction
ObjectiveFully analog interlock that disables the tractive system under IMD, BMS, and BSPD fault conditions, with no cockpit reset and mandatory manual intervention per EV.7.2.3.
ContributionDesigned a three-stage self-latching relay PCB with independent BMS, IMD, and BSPD fault paths using self-holding NO contacts. Any fault latches the corresponding relay stage open regardless of driver input, requiring manual reset at the vehicle per EV.7.2.3.
Outcome<50 ms fault response across 50+ bench tests, 100% detection accuracy, passed FSAE electrical technical inspection, and integrated into the competition HV safety chain.
